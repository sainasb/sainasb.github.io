Our Cause


Ever since Saina was 6, she has found a passion for raising money through donations towards pancreatic cancer research. Saina's grandfather passed away from pancreatic cancer and ever since Saina has made it a point that we need to invest in scientific research that helps in findings cures for pancreatic cancer. For the last 8 years, every birthday instead of simply accepting gifts, she asks for donation money so she can donate to patients with pancreatic cancer. Recently, she has been raising awareness, creating charitable events, and creating custom art pieces impacting the cancer community.
Every penny that gets put into Art To Save a Heart is one more penny added to helping someone struggling with cancer. Here, we make sure that the quality you get is good to spice up your home. Purchasing with us helps a greater cause.
