SAINA BHATT

Painter, Drawer, Illustrator

​The Seattle based artist, Saina Bhatt, has been honing her skills in artistry for the past 13 years. Ever since she could hold a pencil, she uncovered her fascinating passion for drawing and painting leading to her years of art experience. Although originally being focused on animals and wildlife, Saina has recently found a new passion for landscape painting and hopes to continue this path and she experiments with different styles. Saina switches back and forth between painting and drawing and she finds that each technique brings a different kind of life to her art piece resulting in a different feel altogether. Saina's evolution into her skill of art work today allowed her to introduce realism into her pieces to take her range of talent and skill further.
